Comments Policy

You may have noticed a lack of comments on blog posts and other pages here. Let me assure you, this is no mistake: there are no comments on this site.

I don't think it's responsible to create a place for comments and content without a means to moderate it. And frankly, I have no desire to moderate comments. So, no comments!

If you have found a technical problem with the site, I invite you to open an issue on GitHub.

If you need to tell me something, you can find me on a number of social networks, like Twitter. Please note that my DMs are not open.

If you are compelled to respond to something I've written, blog it. And let me know, or don't. That's up to you.

If these options do not work, a good email address to reach me at is holler then the at-sign followed by tattooed dot dev.
